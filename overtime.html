"Overtime" is a thrilling horror game where you play as an office worker trying to escape after a power outage. As darkness envelops the office, you must hide, crouch, and navigate through the environment to avoid a relentless pursuer. With improved monster AI and interactive elements like lockers to hide in, the game promises a suspenseful experience. Explore, survive, and uncover the secrets of your workplace in this engaging beta release.

For more details or to download the game, visit the Overtime itch.io page.
